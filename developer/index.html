Bio

We all seem to reach the point in our lives where we either settle or we step up to the occasion.

For years I was involved in the arts. As a child, I would sketch through the day. Later in life, theatre and music became that artistic focus. But then in school, I found that math and science gave me that same excitement. And like a proper millenial, I found tech early on. I was desperate to not become an accountant or spend my life in school studying science. And after college, I learned that sadly the art world does a lot more taking than giving. I felt stuck.

A few years ago I began learning web development and design. I suddenly felt that I wasn't suppressing any of my interests. It was time to make a change and take this more seriously, so after doing design work for a company I enrolled in the full time Web Development Immersive at General Assembly. After honing in on those skills, I feel alive again.
